# Via Labs MVP — Claude Instructions

## Project

This is a **Next.js 16 / React 19 / TypeScript** project. All frontend work happens here.

## What We're Building

**Via** is a mobile app that helps users take care of their skin. The landing page we're building must communicate this clearly and convert visitors to signups.

### Core product features to highlight:
1. **Face scans & skin analysis** — AI-powered analysis of your skin
2. **Product grading** — ingest your current products, get a score on how well they fit your skincare goals, and get recommendations for gaps
3. **Virtual consultations** — connect with Via's skincare team for personalized routine advice

### Goal of this page:
A highly animated, well-designed, high-converting D2C landing page. Every section should serve conversion. No filler.

## Coding Philosophy — Jason Fried & DHH (Basecamp)

Write code the way Basecamp does:

- **Pragmatic over clever.** Choose the boring, obvious solution. If it works and is readable, ship it.
- **No premature abstraction.** Don't create a component, hook, or utility until you need it in at least two places.
- **Delete before you add.** Fewer moving parts beats more configurability every time.
- **Naming is design.** Clear names > comments. If you need a comment to explain it, rename it.
- **Don't over-engineer animation.** Every animation must earn its place — it should communicate something, not just look cool.
- **No speculative features.** Build what's asked, nothing more.
